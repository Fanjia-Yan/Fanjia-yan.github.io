About

Hi, my name is Fanjia! I am rising junior studying Computer Science and Applied Mathematics at the University of California, Berkeley. My academic interest lies within computer graphics, and algorithmic design, blockchain security, and decentralized finance. You can find my resume here.

I was determined to be Mathematician upon entering the college. I was drawn by the elegance and succinctness of mathematical arguments and theorems. While I immersed myself into an abstracted world of analysis and algebra. I also explored the application of abstracting thinking into real world. On my journey to computer science, I discovered fields of study such as Interactive Proof System from cryptography, Virtual Reality in Computer Graphics, and Knowledge Assessment Algorithm from Human Computer Interaction. I fell attracted to the immense application those technologies enable. With this in mind, I devote myself into research and software engineering to cast the theortical fascinations into the reality we interact with.
